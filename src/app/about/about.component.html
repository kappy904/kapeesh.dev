My family

Born and raised in South Africa, my wife and I have always been adventure seekers. When the opportunity arose to move to Amsterdam just a few months after we got married, we couldn’t pass it up. For the past 2 years, we’ve been living in Amsterdam and exploring Europe along with our darling Daisy, our 2-year-old Pekingese who joined us from Cape Town. Also, Daisy has her own Instagram page...Yes, we’re those people!

Travel

I have travelled and explored South Africa from a young age, but my first international trip was actually for my honeymoon, and that’s when the travel bug bit! My wife and I are proud foodies and that is what fuels our love for travel- discovering and indulging in the various local cuisines of the places we visit.
